About KSF

Kashmore Students Federation (KSF) at MUET SZAB Campus was formally established in 2016 by Rashid Ali Bajkani and his peers. KSF welcomes new students every year and holds farewell events for graduating batches. KSF follows a collective leadership model where every student contributes according to their skills; senior batches help guide decisions after consulting juniors.

Vision & Mission

To unite the students of Kashmore District under one banner of brotherhood, equality, and leadership at MUET SZAB Campus. We empower every student to contribute through their skills, ideas, and teamwork to build a respectful and progressive student community.
